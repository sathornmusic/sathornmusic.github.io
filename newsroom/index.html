Sathorn 1.1 Now Available

March 19, 2019 (Bangkok, Thailand)
Download Press Kit
Sathron 1.1 is out, and it's the first major feature update to Sathorn. Version 1.1 allows users to choose their own custom theme color for the app, as they are now no longer restricted to just the 7 default theme colors. It also includes a Today Extension for users to quickly love/dislike songs. You can view the updated app in the App Store here.


Sathorn 1.0.5 released

March 14, 2019 (Bangkok, Thailand)
Download Press Kit
Sathron 1.0.5 was released today, which fixes a few bugs and adds a few small features. Notably, on the Songs tab, the slideout menu now has a "Love" option for quickly Loving a song. You can also now sort Apple Music playlists by 5 sort options--Artist, Album, Release Date (Newest), Release Date (Oldest), and Default. You can view the app in the App Store here.


Sathorn 1.0 released

March 12, 2019 (Bangkok, Thailand)
Download Press Kit
I am pleased to announce that version 1.0 of Sathorn, an Apple Music client for iOS, is now available to download in the iOS App Store. Sathorn is the most fully featured Apple Music client in the App Store, and is the only app with the ability to playback Apple Music Radio. You can view the app in the App Store here.
